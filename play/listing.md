## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Subtitle Player is a tiny, focused app for reading movie subtitles on your
phone while the film plays on a TV, projector, or laptop that doesn't have the
subtitles you need.

Load a subtitle file, press play the moment the movie starts, and follow along.
If the subtitles drift out of sync with the dialogue, nudge them back into place
in half-second steps.

It does one thing well: subtitles, and only subtitles. No video, no streaming,
no account, no ads.

FEATURES
• Opens standard .srt and .vtt subtitle files
• Big, readable current line with a preview of what's coming next
• Press play when the movie starts; scrub or jump 10 seconds to line things up
• Fine sync adjustment (-0.5s / +0.5s) for when subtitles drift
• Keeps the screen awake while playing
• Full-screen immersive mode that hides the system bars
• Lock to portrait or landscape, or let it rotate freely
• Hide the controls for a clean, subtitles-only view
• Optionally show or hide the upcoming line

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything runs on your device. Your subtitle files are never uploaded, and the
app collects no data, needs no network connection, and requests no unnecessary
permissions.
